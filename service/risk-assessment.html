Risk Assessment

The Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005 requires responsible persons to undertake a Fire Risk Assessment of the Premises you are responsible for.

Types of premises that require assessments are:

  • Offices & Shops
  • Premises that provide care, including care homes and hospitals
  • Community halls, places of worship and other community premises
  • The shared areas in properties several householders live in
  • Pubs, clubs and restaurants
  • Schools and sports centres
  • Tents and marquees
  • Hotels and hostels
  • Factories and warehouses.
