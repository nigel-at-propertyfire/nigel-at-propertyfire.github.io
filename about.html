About PFP

Established in 2013, Property Fire Protection Ltd, deliver complete fire safety solutions

Due to our highly regarded reputation within the industry and expertise installing fire safety solutions, much of our work is sourced through referrals. We operate within London, the Home Counties and beyond, delivering high quality, professional results at competitive prices to clients across a range of industries and contract sizes.

Providing A Comprehensive Service

Our comprehensive service includes the supply, installation and maintenance of a variety of fire safety equipment, including radio and wired alarmed systems, aspirating systems, emergency lighting, fire door surveys and fire extinguishers.

Our Services

Always Up To Standard

As a business we are confident we meet the demanding and changing needs in fire safety legislation, conforming to British Standard Specifications; providing our customers with the very best in service and support.
